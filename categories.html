TAP CATEGORIES






CATEGORY A1

Players with optimal functional mobility with motor impairment below the knee or motor impairment of one upper limb. E.g.-a player who has an amputation or other disability below the knee or a player who has an amputation of one arm at joint level or who displays a congenital malformation in one arm.


CATEGORY A

Players with optimal functional mobility with impairment above the knee of one or both lower extremities and with or without alterations in one or two upper extremities. E.g.-amputation above the knee, hemiplegic, hemiparesis, congenital malformation and cerebral palsy.


CATEGORY B

Player with an intermediate functional mobility that can play on a 60 x 21 foot tennis court using an orange ball.


CATEGORY C

Player with low functional disability that can play on a 36 x 18 foot tennis court with a red ball.
